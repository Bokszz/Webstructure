  • Thank you for your interest in St. Augustine Catholic School.

    Admission to St. Augustine Catholic School is open to girls and boys in Pre-K through 8th grade. We have openings for the 2019-2020 school year. Tuition starts at $6,250 for one student, making our school one of the most affordable in the Archdiocese of Washington. 

    WHY CONSIDER ST. AUGUSTINE?

    • We have a strong record of high school placement at outstanding schools like Academy of Holy Cross, Archbishop Carroll, Bishop McNamara, DeMatha, Elizabeth Seton, Georgetown Day School, Georgetown Visitation, St.John’s, St. Vincent Palotti, and Stone Ridge. More than 70 percent of graduates attend Catholic high schools.

    • St. Augustine School has three religious sisters from the Handmaids of the Holy Child Jesus serving on its staff.

    • Parents have the opportunity to actively participate in your child’s education through volunteer and service opportunities.

    • Our teacher/student ratio of 18:1.

    • St. Augustine Catholic School follows the Admission Policy as defined by the Archdiocese of Washington. Students are considered without regard to race, ethnicity, religion or national origin.
